The 360-degree appraisal system is used in organizations for various purposes, including: Performance evaluation: The feedback from multiple sources provides a comprehensive view of the employee's strengths and weaknesses, which can be used to evaluate their overall performance. Employee development: The feedback can be used to identify areas for improvement and to develop a plan for the employee's career growth and development. Succession planning: The 360-degree appraisal can be used to identify high-potential employees and to plan for future leadership and management positions. Compensation and rewards: The results of the 360-degree appraisal can be used to inform decisions about promotions, transfers, and compensation. Improving communication and teamwork: The feedback from peers, subordinates, and customers can help employees understand how they are perceived by others and how they can improve their communication and collaboration skills. Overall, the 360-degree appraisal system is a valuable tool for organizations that are looking to improve employee performance, develop their talent, and support their overall business goals.



Objectives
