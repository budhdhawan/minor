About this Project

360-degree appraisal is a performance evaluation method that collects feedback from multiple sources to provide a comprehensive view of an employee's strengths and areas for improvement. It typically involves feedback from the employee's direct supervisor, peers, subordinates, and even customers. The goal of 360-degree appraisal is to provide a well-rounded and accurate assessment of the employee's performance, which can be used for development purposes, to identify training needs, and to support decision-making about promotions, transfers, and compensation.
